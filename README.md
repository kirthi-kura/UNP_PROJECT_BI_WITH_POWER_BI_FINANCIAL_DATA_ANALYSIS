# UNP_PROJECT_BI_WITH_POWER_BI_FINANCIAL_DATA_ANALYSIS

In collaboration with UNP Education, I completed a Power BI project focused on analyzing a retail company’s financial dataset and converting raw data into actionable business intelligence. The objective of the project was to evaluate overall financial performance and deliver strategic insights through structured data analysis and visualization. I initiated the project by performing comprehensive data cleaning and validation to ensure the accuracy, consistency, and integrity of financial records. Post data preparation, I calculated critical financial metrics such as Gross Margin and Loss and developed advanced DAX measures to enable dynamic and in-depth analysis.  I established key performance indicators including Total Sales, Total Cost, Total Discount, Total Gross Margin, Total Profit, Total Loss, Total Manufacturing Cost, Total Units Sold, Profit per Unit, and Cost per Unit to assess operational efficiency and profitability. I subsequently designed an interactive dashboard titled “Orion Enterprises Financial Performance Analysis Dashboard,” providing a consolidated view of the organization’s financial health. The dashboard incorporated advanced visualizations such as trend charts and geographic maps to evaluate sales performance, product profitability, and regional contributions. The project concluded with the identification of performance gaps and the delivery of data-driven recommendations, demonstrating the practical application of business intelligence tools in supporting informed decision-making and strategic financial improvement.
